Medi Mind AI – Complete System Workflow

Vision: An intelligent, centralized health monitoring platform.

The Backbone: A Local SQLite Database (vitals.db) serves as the single source of truth, ensuring data privacy and offline accessibility.

Key Pillars:

Vitals Management: Quantitative health monitoring.

Symptom Intelligence: AI-driven diagnostic assistance.

Conversational Concierge: Real-time, context-aware AI support.


1.Intelligent Vitals Tracking


The Input: Users log quantitative metrics (Heart Rate, Blood Pressure, SpO2, etc.).

The Process:

Data is securely saved to the local SQLite DB.

The system generates automated AI Health Insights.

The Outcome:

Visual trend charts help users understand their health trajectory over time.

Personalized health scores based on consistent tracking.


2. AI Symptom Analyzer


User Inputs: Body part selection, categorical tags, and severity sliders.

The AI Engine:

Backend POST request sends structured symptom data to our AI model.

Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash for rapid, accurate interpretation.

The Value Add:

Generates differential diagnosis JSON.

Instantly calculates urgency levels.

Provides actionable recommendations via a simplified dashboard.

3. Conversational AI Chatbot


Intelligent History: The chatbot compiles previous conversation history to ensure continuity.

Process Flow:

User inputs a specific query or selects a topic.

Gemini 2.5 Flash (chat model) processes the request.

Markdown-formatted responses are rendered for clear, readable advice.

Activity Feed: Users can review recent interaction history to stay informed.

4.Safety First – Emergency Protocol


Emergency Logic: Every query passes through a safety filter before AI processing.

The Override:

If Medical Emergency Detected: The system triggers an immediate Emergency Alert Override.

The Action: Bypasses standard chatbot flows to display critical instructions (Call 911).
